Why will you visit Taraz?

Taraz (Kazakh: Тараз, تاراز, romanized: Taraz (audio speaker iconlisten); known to Europeans as Talas) is a city and the administrative center of Jambyl Region in Kazakhstan, located on the Talas (Taraz) River in the south of the country near the border with Kyrgyzstan. It had a population of 330,100 as of the 1999 census, up 9% from 1989, making it one of the fastest-growing cities in the country, after Nur-Sultan and Turkistan.
